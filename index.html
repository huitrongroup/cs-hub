Common Customer Issues
Click to expand — decision tree + ready-to-send template inside each
📦 Order hasn't arrived
▶
Decision Steps
1
Check estimated delivery date. Still within window? → Advise patience + share tracking.
2
Past delivery date? Check carrier tracking status.
3
Tracking says Delivered? → Ask to check mailbox / neighbors / building office.
4
Tracking stuck / no updates? → Offer refund or replacement. File claim if needed.
Copy-Paste Template
Hi [Name]! Thank you for reaching out. I completely understand how worrying this can be! I'm looking into your order right now. Your tracking number is [tracking#] — the last update shows [status]. Orders can sometimes experience small delays. If it hasn't arrived by [date+2 days], please message me right back and I'll make it right immediately. Thank you so much for your patience! 🧡
💸 Customer wants a refund
▶
Decision Steps
1
Was the item personalized? → Refund NOT allowed (policy). Offer alternative.
2
Item damaged or wrong item sent? → Refund approved. Process immediately.
3
Order lost in transit? → Refund approved.
4
Buyer changed mind / not shipped? → Cancel + refund OK. Already shipped? → Not eligible.
Refund Approved Template
Hi [Name]! I'm so sorry about the issue with your order. I've gone ahead and processed a full refund of [amount] to your original payment method. Please allow 3–5 business days for it to appear. We truly appreciate your patience and hope to make this right. Thank you for giving us the chance to resolve this! 🧡
🔄 Customer wants a replacement
▶
Decision Steps
1
Item damaged on arrival? → Replacement approved. Request photo proof.
2
Wrong item sent? → Replacement approved. Ship correct item ASAP.
3
Item lost in transit? → Replacement approved after 7+ days no update.
4
Buyer changed mind? → Not eligible for replacement.
Copy-Paste Template
Hi [Name]! I'm so sorry about this — that is absolutely not the experience we want for you! I've gone ahead and arranged a replacement to be sent to you right away. You'll receive a new shipping confirmation as soon as it's on its way. Thank you so much for your patience — we truly appreciate you! 🧡
✏️ Customer wants to change personalization
▶
Decision Steps
1
Has production started? Check order queue / production status.
2
Not yet in production: → Update details, confirm with customer.
3
Already in production: → Cannot change. Explain policy.
4
Customer upset? Offer discount on new order as goodwill gesture.
Copy-Paste Template
Hi [Name]! Thank you for your order — we want to make sure everything is absolutely perfect! Could you please confirm the following details? [details needed]. Once I have this confirmed, I'll get started right away. We're so excited to create this for you! 🧡
📍 Customer entered wrong shipping address
▶
Decision Steps
1
Has the order shipped yet?
2
Not shipped: Update address immediately. Confirm new address with customer.
3
Already shipped: Contact carrier — redirect may be possible for a fee.
4
If redirect not possible: Wait for return, then reship to correct address.
Copy-Paste Template
Hi [Name]! No worries — let's get this sorted! If your order hasn't shipped yet I can update the address right now. Could you please confirm the correct address? [address]. I'll take care of this immediately. If it's already on its way, I'll do my best to redirect it. Thank you for catching this! 🧡
💔 Item arrived damaged
▶
Decision Steps
1
Request photo of damage immediately.
2
Minor damage (cosmetic)? → Offer partial refund or discount on next order.
3
Major damage (unusable)? → Full refund or replacement — customer's choice.
4
Document damage for carrier claim if shipping-related.
Copy-Paste Template
Hi [Name]! Oh no, I'm so sorry your item arrived damaged — that's absolutely not okay and I'm going to fix this right away! Could you please send a photo of the damage? Once I see it, I'll immediately arrange either a full replacement or a complete refund — whichever you prefer. I'm so sorry again! 🧡

💸 Refund Policy
✓ Allowed if
Item arrived damaged
Wrong item was sent
Order lost in transit
Order not yet shipped (cancellation)
✗ Not allowed if
Custom item already produced
Buyer simply changed mind
Order already shipped (non-damage)
Violates shop policy
🔄 Replacement Policy
✓ Allowed if
Item arrived damaged (with photo)
Wrong item was sent
Item lost in transit 7+ days
✗ Not allowed if
Buyer changed mind
Personalization error by buyer
Item delivered as described
✏️ Personalization Policy
✓ Can edit if
Not yet in production
Change requested within 24h
Minor spelling correction
✗ Cannot edit if
Production already started
Item already shipped
Major redesign requested
🚚 Shipping Policy
✓ Within scope
Update address before shipment
Provide tracking on request
Reship after confirmed return
✗ Outside scope
Guarantee carrier delivery time
Change address after shipment
Refund shipping on delivered orders

When to Escalate
Stop and pass up the chain — don't resolve alone
Escalate immediately if
Customer is threatening a negative review or public complaint
Customer mentions legal action, disputes, or chargebacks
High-value order ($150+) with disputed outcome
Customer is extremely hostile or abusive in messages
Unusual or unclear situation not covered by policy
Repeated contact about the same unresolved issue
Potential shipping fraud or suspicious order patterns
